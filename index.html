Welcome,

This is a simple tool for editing your .Xdefaults. The goal of this was to create a quicker way to update my urxvt theme. Another way (for emacs users) would be to open it up in "rainbox-mode". Depending on your distro you may want to be editing your ".xResources" file instead. Go Ahead and Paste a file into this window, upload your ~.Xdefaults, or load the sample file.
